Japanese Translations

Ever wonder what the Japanese words for this game are? Look no further! After coming across a walkthrough for the Japanese version of the game, I made this list of the literal translations of the Japanese words and how they're interpreted into the English version. Plese keep in mind that I couldn't find out what ALL the words meant. If you do know or have an idea, please let me know!

Yes, yes, I KNOW the Japanese language does not have an "L" in it, but these are the literal translations fixed to pronounce like we would in the English Language. That is, if you were to translate the language and prounounce it phonetically, it would SOUND that way. For instance, if you were to translate the Japanese Kanji for "Serdio", it would sound more like "Seldio" or (Se-ru-dio"), with the tongue hitting the top of the mouth so it sounds like an "L" rather than an "R", but when it was put into the English version of the game, they changed it to "Serdio"....does that make any sense? o.O Probably not, but that's what happens when I try to explain things...

Thanks to Halloweengurl for giving me some more words and translations!
