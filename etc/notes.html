My screen keeps blacking out right after (insert battle here)!! I've tried doing it like 20 times but it always happens!! What gives?

  • Ah yes, the "Dreaded Blackout Screen". Are you playing the game on the PS2, by chance? If so, then try to play the game on a regular PlayStation. There are some parts in the game (the one I hear most frequently is the Lenus/Regole battle) where the PS2 laser reads the disc improperly, causing a blackout screen. Playing the game on the PS will solve this problem. Also try cleaning your disc with a kit like Disc Doctor, especially if you bought the game used.
