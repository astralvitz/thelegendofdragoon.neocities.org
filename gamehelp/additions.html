Additions

Here you will find a list of everyone's additions at each level. This way, you'll know which Additions give you more SP, which give you more Power, or which do both. Keep in mind that the only way to get the FINAL Addition is to get to Level 5 (80+ complete Additions) on all of that character's previous Additions.

For everyone, here are the levels for Additions:
Level 1: 0-19
Level 2: 20-39
Level 3: 40-59
Level 4: 60-79
Level 5: 80-99
