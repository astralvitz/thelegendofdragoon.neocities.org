Item List

Items are one of the most important elements to any RPG. They can heal your party or attack your enemy, or even give you special attributes such as an increase in strength or speed. Listed below are all the items found in The Legend of Dragoon, categorized by Recovering Items, Attacking Items, Curing Items, and Attributing Items.
