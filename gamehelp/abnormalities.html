Abnormalities

Abnormalities are caused by attacks from enemies and causes your character to lose HP, SP, attack power or even total control of his/her body. Also, some Abnormalities are cured when the battle is over, or when a certain enemy is defeated. Going into Dragoon Mode can cure all Abnormalities (except Unconciousness) and prevents abnormalites (again, except Unconciousness).
