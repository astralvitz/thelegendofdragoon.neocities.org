Weapons and Armor

There are many weapons and armor in the land of Endiness. Some of the armor all the characters can wear, but weapons are made specifically for one character. Here is a list of most of the weapons and armor in the game, which character they are for, what they do, and where you can find them.

Yeah, I said "most". I haven't bought all the weapons/armor in the game, mainly because I haven't needed to. However, that might change in the future. ;) Also keep in mind that you can get some of these cool items from enemies too!

Sometimes, two locations are listed. This is because you can find certain weapons/armor in treasure chests in some areas and stores in others.

Thanks to Dale Ray for some additional information! :D
