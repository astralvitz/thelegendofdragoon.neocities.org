Stardust Picture Guide

Here is a complete list of all the Stardust in Endiness, now with pictures to show you the precise locations of each. Those marked with an "-->" indicate steps in the process of getting the Stardust. Stardust is needed to help Martel's daughter, Lil, recover from her illness. There are fifty Stardusts in all, and after every ten you get a really cool prize from Martel. Even if you forget one, you can always go back and get it anytime before you land on the Divine Tree on the fourth disc.
